Understanding The Pity System

  • Hard Pity: You are guaranteed to get a 5-star character within 90 pulls of your last 5-star pull.
  • Soft Pity: The chance of getting a 5-star character dramatically increases after ~75 pulls.
  • The 50/50: When you pull a 5-star, there's a 50% chance it will be the limited banner character, and a 50% chance it will be a standard banner character.
  • Guaranteed Character: If you lose the 50/50 and get a standard character, your next 5-star pull is 100% guaranteed to be the limited banner character.

Top-Up Bonus

  • Oneiric Shards: Honkai: Star Rail's premium equivalent to Stellar Jades. They can be purchased in packs of 60, 300, 980, 1980, 3280, and 6480.
  • First Recharge Bonus: The first time you purchase an Oneiric Shard pack, the amount of currency you obtain from it is doubled. In this calculator, oneiric packs with a double bonus will be highlighted.
